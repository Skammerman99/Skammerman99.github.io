Robotics

I have a wealth of experience with robotics, from my four years in high school as a programmer for FRC to founding the Zotbotics Robotics Club at UCI. I worked on computer vision, autonomous routines, and direct robot control.

Game Dev

I've worked multiple games including The Secret Word Garden, the personal project of an Intellivision developer, and Royal Rushdown, an infinite runner created at a Game Jam. I dream of making a pizza-themed Metroidvania game someday.

Shoutcasting

My love for video games enabled me to find a job as a shoutcaster at UCI Esports. Over the last three years, I've casted League of Legends, Overwatch, Smash Bros., and more at both collegiate and high-school levels for UCI's Twitch livestreams.
